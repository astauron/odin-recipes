Creamy Tomato Chicken Pasta

A simple, herby, creamy tomato & chicken pasta dish

This is an pasta dish which will please everyone. An easy and simple dish that cooks in around 30-40 mins, with very little preparation with ingredients that are easy to find and you may already have.

This is a very easy dish to customise as well, feel free to substitue the chicken or even add in mushrooms!

This recipe is for 4 people, but feel free to half the ingredients to make it for 2.

Ingredients

  • 4x Garlic Clove
  • 60g Mature Cheddar Cheese
  • 500g Diced Chicken Breast
  • 3g Dried Oregano
  • 350g Pasta (Penne is recommended but anything will do)
  • 400g Tomato Passata
  • 20g Chicken Stock Paste
  • 80g Baby Spinach
  • 150g Double Cream
  • 1tsp Sugar
  • 150ml Water

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil with 1/2tsp of salt.
  2. Heat a large frying pan with some oil at a medium high heat.
  3. Peel and grate the garlic and set to the side.
  4. Grate the cheese and set to the side.
  5. When your frying pan is hot, add the diced chicken. Fry until golden brown (8-10 minutes).
  6. When your water is boiling add the pasta. Cook until tender (10-12 minutes).
  7. When the chicken is golden brown (and no longer pink in the middle) add the garlic and oregano for 1 minute.
  8. When the pasta is cooked, drain in a colander and return to the saucepan. Stir through a small amount of oil to stop it sticking together.
  9. Add the passata, chicken stock paste, sugar and the water to the chicken. Bring to a boil, and lower the heat to medium.
  10. When the sauce thickens stir in the baby spinach one handful at a time until wilted.
  11. Once all the spinach is wilted, stir in the double cream and the cheese.
  12. Add the pasta to the chicken and sauce and stir together.
  13. Serve to bowls.
