Bulgogi Pork Noodles

A quick and tasty noodle dish

A korean sweet and smokey noodle dish thats is as delicious as it is quick and easy to make. No more than 15 minutes and you'll be enjoying a lovely dinner.

This recipe is for 2 people, but feel free to double the ingredients to make it for 4.

Ingredients

  • 240g Pork Mince (Beef Mince can be used instead)
  • 80g Sliced Mushrooms
  • 1tsp Thai Spice Mix
  • 120g Coleslaw Mix
  • 125g Egg Noodles
  • 75g Bulgogi Sauce
  • 15ml Soy Sauce
  • 5g Roasted Sesame Seeds
  • 2tbsp Tomato Ketchup

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil with 1/2tsp of salt.
  2. Heat a large frying pan with some oil at a medium high heat.
  3. When your frying pan is hot, add the pork mince, mushrooms, thai spice mix and the coleslaw mix. Fry for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Add the egg noodles to the boiling water.
  5. Once cooked drain the egg noodles and run under cold water.
  6. Drain the fat from the pork and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Stir in the bulgogi sauce, soy sauce and the ketchup.
  8. Add the egg noodles to the frying pan and toss to coat.
  9. Heat everything up. Taste and add salt and pepper if you'd like. Add a splash of water if needed.
  10. Serve to bowls.
