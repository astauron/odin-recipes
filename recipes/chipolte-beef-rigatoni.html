Chipotle Beef Rigatoni

A simple, slightly spicy pasta dish

This pasta dish is super simple to make and has a slight bit of spice to it. A perfect family meal

This recipe is for 4 people, but feel free to half the ingredients to make it for 2.

Ingredients

  • 2x Garlic Cloves
  • 360g Pasta (Rigatoni is recommended but anything will do)
  • 480g Beef Mince
  • 40g Chipotle Paste (Add less if you dont want as much heat)
  • 400g Tomato Passata
  • 20g Beef Stock Paste
  • 60g Mature Cheddar Cheese
  • 5g Roasted Sesame Seeds
  • 100g Baby Spinach
  • 2tsp Sugar
  • 200ml Water

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil with 1/2tsp of salt.
  2. Heat a large frying pan with some oil at a medium high heat.
  3. Peel and grate the garlic and set to the side.
  4. Grate the cheese and set to the side.
  5. When your water is boiling add the pasta. Cook until tender (10-12 minutes).
  6. When your frying pan is hot, add the beef mince. Fry until browned (5-6 minutes).
  7. When the pasta is cooked, drain in a colander and return to the saucepan. Stir through a small amount of oil to stop it sticking together.
  8. When the beef mince is browned add the garlic and chipotle paste and stir fry 1 minute.
  9. Add the passata, beef stock paste, sugar and the water. Simmer until the sauce has thickened.
  10. When the sauce has thickened stir in the baby spinach one handful at a time until wilted.
  11. Once all the spinach is wilted, stir in the pasta and three quaters of the cheese.
  12. Serve to bowls.
